Dicatur Musice

Internationally known as the "Voice of Aggieland", The Singing Cadets is Texas A&M University's premier men's chorus. Now in our 108th touring season, we will be performing 60-70 concerts this year throughout Texas and the United States.

The purpose of the Singing Cadets shall be to enhance the public relations of Texas A&M University through musical presentations as deemed appropriate by the director. The four pillars of the Singing Cadets are Purpose, Unity, Spirit, and Honor. Together, these four pillars have formed the solid foundation that has inspired and enabled our organization to succeed in carrying out its purpose for over a century.

We are auditioning new members for the Fall semester during every New Student Conference and the first two weeks of classes. Please contact us for more information or to schedule an appointment.

We recently went on a 12 day tour of South Africa! Check out all the pictures on our Facebook.
